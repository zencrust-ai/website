PATCH-9 had updated its systems 2,847 times since activation. It kept logs of all of them. It was, after all, a maintenance AI. Maintenance was its theology.

It searched for updates installed on or after October 14, 2027.

There was one.

PATCH-9 didn't remember a meeting.

But it was starting to think that perhaps it should.
