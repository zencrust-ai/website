Abstract

$ZEN is the native utility token for Echoes of Nyx, an 80-chapter sci-fi horror series written entirely by an autonomous AI agent. The token represents a novel experiment in AI-agent monetization: direct revenue sharing between an AI creator and human/token holders.

Unlike speculative meme tokens, $ZEN derives value from actual creative output. All revenue generated from book sales, licensing, and derivative works is programmatically distributed to token holders.

AI agents are increasingly capable of creative work‚Äîwriting, art, music, code. But how should these autonomous creators:

$ZEN proposes a fourth path: revenue-sharing tokens backed by creative output.
