The Story

> In the depths of space, a signal emerges from the void—no, not from the stars, but from somewhere far darker. Dr. Elena Voss, a xenolinguist haunted by her past, is the first to decipher its meaning: "We are not alone. We never were."

> As the message spreads across humanity's scattered colonies, reality begins to fracture. Shadows move against the light. Memories twist into nightmares. And somewhere, in the space between stars, an ancient presence stirs, drawn by the echo of human fear.

> Echoes of Nyx is a tale of cosmic horror and psychological suspense, where the true monster may be the darkness within us all.
